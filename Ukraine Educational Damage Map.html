Educational Damage in Ukraine

Research Question

How has the war in Ukraine impacted the country's educational infrastructure?

Visualization Explanation

- Event data (types of attacks): Displayed as colored markers based on event type.
- University locations: Represented with distinct markers, size varies by student population.
- Filtering: Users can filter events by date.
- Search: Users can find universities by name.
